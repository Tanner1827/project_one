A Few People Who Inspire Me

    .

    Trey Flanigan

    What I like about the work of Trey Flanigan is his stylistic use of thick, dark line work and minimal color palettes, which gives his illustrations a strong and recognizable look. His characters and objects are very expressive, using exaggerated shapes and playful proportions to create personality and energy. Overall, his work feels bold, simple, and visually engaging while still maintaining a clear and confident style.

    .

    Robert Beatty

    Robert Beatty creates a strong sense of depth and movement through his use of shadowing, thoughtful color choices, and detailed imagery. His work often feels layered and immersive, pulling the viewer deeper into the composition. These elements help give his designs a surreal and visually striking quality.

    .

    Pavement

    Pavement is an Indie, slacker-rock band that was formed in the late 80s, early 90s, and has definitely changed how I listen to music. They have so many cool chord progressions,deep lyrics, and amazing album art that have inspired me creatively for a long time.
