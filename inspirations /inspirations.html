A Few People Who Inspire Me

.

Trey Flanigan

What I like about this artist is his stylistic use of thick dark line work and minimal colors, aswell as expressive characters and objects.

.

Robert Beatty

He creates beautiful depth and movement through the use of shadowing, thoughtful colors, and detailed imagery.

.

Pavement

Pavement is an Indie, slacker-rock band that was formed in the late 80s, early 90s, and has definitely changed how I listen to music. They have so many cool chord progressions and deep lyrics that have inspired me artistically and musically for a long time.
